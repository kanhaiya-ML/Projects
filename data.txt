1. SCOPE & DEFINITIONS
This policy applies to all purchases made on the Flipkart platform.
- “Customer/Buyer”: Person placing the order.
- “Seller”: Third‑party merchant listing the product.
- “Order”: A confirmed purchase request.
- “Dispatch”: Handing over the product to the courier partner.
- “Return Window”: Period from date of delivery within which a return/replacement can be requested.

2. CANCELLATION POLICY
2.1 Standard Orders
- An order can be cancelled anytime BEFORE it is dispatched.
- AFTER dispatch / during delivery: Cancellation not allowed; the customer may refuse delivery at the doorstep.
- Category‑specific time windows may apply (see product page). Once the window expires, cancellation may not be free.
- If a cancellation fee applies, the amount will be shown on the order confirmation page.
- Flipkart reserves the right to accept or reject any cancellation, and to modify the time window or fee.

2.2 Hyperlocal (MINUTES Delivery) Orders
- Self‑serve cancellation is NOT available for MINUTES orders due to rapid dispatch.
- Cancellation/refund can only be requested via Customer Support (CX Agent) under these circumstances:
    a) Order not delivered within the estimated time shown at order placement.
    b) Order not yet picked up by the delivery partner.
    c) Seller has not accepted or has cancelled the order for reasons not attributable to the buyer.
    d) Any other reason that Flipkart may update from time to time.

2.3 Cancellation by Seller / Flipkart
- Sellers or Flipkart may cancel an order (in whole or part) due to:
    • Product unavailability
    • Force majeure (unforeseeable events)
    • Suspected fraudulent activity
    • Violation of Terms of Use
    • Logistical constraints
- If cancelled by the platform/seller, the buyer is NOT charged.
- For prepaid orders, a full refund is initiated. For cancellations by seller, a full refund is guaranteed.
- Refund tracking is available on the Order Details page.

3. RETURN POLICY
General Note: The return policy on the individual product page overrides the general policy. All products in a category may not have the same return options.

3.1 General Rules for All Returns
- Returns must be requested within the return window.
- Products must be unused, undamaged, with all original accessories, manuals, warranty cards, and packaging.
- For mobile/laptop/tablet: disable screen lock, remove iCloud lock, and format the device before return.
- Open Box Delivery: If a product is different/damaged at the time of open box acceptance, a refund (including on‑spot for COD) will be given. After acceptance, no return for non‑defective reasons.

3.3 Part 2 – Returns Pick‑Up & Validation
- Pick‑up address can be changed only if service is available at the new address.
- During pick‑up, the product is checked for:
    • Correct Product: IMEI/name/image/brand/serial/barcode/MRP tag intact.
    • Complete Product: All in‑box accessories, freebies, combos present.
    • Unused Product: No stains, unwashed, seal/return tags intact. For electronics, device formatted and screen/iCloud lock disabled.
    • Undamaged Product: No scratches, dents, tears, holes on body/ports/SIM tray/back panel.
    • Undamaged Packaging: Original box undamaged.
- If any condition fails, the return pickup will be refused.

3.4 Part 3 – Successful Return & Exceptions
- If seller cannot replace, a refund will be given.
- Missing/damaged accessory → replacement of accessory or e‑GV of equivalent amount at seller’s discretion.
- Open box delivery: If wrong/damaged product received, refund given on‑spot (COD). After acceptance, only manufacturing defect claims allowed, subject to category policies.
- For Flipkart‑installed products: do not open box yourself; authorised personnel will unbox & install.
- Furniture: faulty parts replaced first; full replacement only if part replacement cannot resolve issue (as assessed by service personnel).
- Flipkart may limit number of returns per order unit after evaluation.
- Exceptions covered by separate validation: a) product undelivered, b) product/accessories missing, c) wrong product/accessories delivered.

4. REPLACEMENT & REPAIR POLICY (SERVICE CENTRE)
For certain brands (Mobiles, Electronics, Large Appliances listed in sections G, H, I, J), warranty and defect resolution is provided by brand‑authorized service centres.
- Customers must contact the brand service centre directly.
- For Samsung, Dead‑On‑Arrival (DOA) certificate from brand must be shared with Flipkart support.
- Flipkart acts as a marketplace; final decision on replacement/repair rests with the seller/brand.
- You can locate authorized service partners via Flipkart’s help section.

5. REFUND POLICY
- Refund is initiated once the returned product is received and inspected by the seller.
- Refund timelines:
    • Prepaid orders (Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, UPI, Wallet): 5‑7 business days after return pickup/approval.
    • Cash on Delivery (COD): refund to bank account (via NEFT) or as Flipkart Gift Card (eGV), within 5‑7 business days. For open box delivery instant refunds, cash may be handed over or transferred depending on the mode.
- Refund status can be tracked on the Order Details page.
- For cancelled orders (by seller/Flipkart), refund is processed within 5‑7 business days.
- In cases of missing accessory, an e‑Gift Voucher (eGV) equivalent to the accessory price may be issued.

6. PAYMENT METHODS
Flipkart accepts the following payment methods:
- Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, RuPay)
- Debit Cards (all major banks)
- Net Banking (50+ banks supported)
- UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM, and other UPI apps)
- Wallets (PhonePe wallet, Paytm, Freecharge, etc.)
- Flipkart Pay Later (buy now, pay later with partner NBFCs)
- Cash on Delivery (COD) – available for selected pin codes and order amounts
- Flipkart Gift Card (eGV) – can be redeemed partially/fully
- EMI options (Credit Card EMI, Debit Card EMI, Bajaj Finserv No‑Cost EMI, etc.)

Payment Security: All transactions are secured via PCI‑DSS compliant payment gateways. Sensitive card data is not stored on Flipkart servers.

7. SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY
- Delivery times vary by location and product. Estimated delivery date is shown on the product and order confirmation pages.
- Standard delivery: 2‑7 business days for most items. Large appliances/furniture may require 7‑14 days.
- Express/MINUTES delivery: within 30‑90 minutes (select pin codes).
- Shipping charges, if any, are displayed at checkout. Many products qualify for free delivery.
- Delivery attempts: Normally 3 attempts. After failed attempts, the order may be returned to seller and refund initiated.
- For large items, in‑home delivery and installation may be included.

8. WARRANTY & GUARANTEE
- Products carry manufacturer/brand warranty (duration mentioned on product page).
- Warranty claims are handled by brand‑authorized service centres.
- Flipkart does not provide a separate warranty unless specified (e.g., extended warranty plans sold separately).
- Refurbished products come with seller/warranty partner warranty; contact details are provided in the packaging.
